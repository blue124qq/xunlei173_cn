Meet the cats

Rescue Is Our Favourite Breed

At the moment, we have fewer cats than usual to rehome and currently have no kittens, so it might take a little longer for us to find you a new pet. Register your details with us and we’ll be in touch if we find a suitable match for you. Although it might take a little more time and patience – rescues are always worth the wait.

One of the best ways you can show your support for rescue cats is to make a donation. We’re truly grateful for anything you can spare.
