Sign up to receive The Battersea Way email

Introducing The Battersea Way's pet advice email! Sign up to receive Battersea emails and be among the first to receive The Battersea Way, our newsletter with everything pet advice from behaviour tips to DIY recipes. You will also receive our current email newsletters with updates on all things Battersea, details of how to support our work, and upcoming events.

Your privacy rights

The data you’ve provided will be used by Battersea Dogs & Cats Home and Battersea Dogs’ Home Ltd to send you emails about pet advice, behind the scenes news, updates about our animals, general information about rehoming, volunteering and various ways you can support Battersea, including fundraising, events and campaigning. You’re free to change your mind at any time. To help us better understand our supporters, and to tailor our communication with you, we may analyse the information you provide. We sometimes use information from public sources. If you’d like to change the way we process your data, let us know by contacting Supporter Services on: Phone: 0300 323 1216 Email: supporterservices@xunlei173.cn Post: Supporter Services Team, Battersea Dogs & Cats Home, 4 Battersea Park Road, London SW8 4AA. Further information about how we protect and use your personal data is set out in our Privacy Policy.
