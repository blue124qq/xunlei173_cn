Get Your Free Rescue Animal Colouring Book

Enjoy colouring in some of these memorable Battersea characters, and many more, with our free downloadable colouring book. Suitable for kids and adults, the Battersea colouring book can be downloaded and coloured in by hand or on a tablet. Packed full of stories from some of our most memorable rescues, as well as beautiful colouring sheets and information about how you can support Battersea’s work, it’s the perfect activity for kids and adults alike.

Your Privacy Rights

The data you’ve provided will be used by Battersea Dogs & Cats Home to send you your colouring book. To help us better understand our supporters, and to tailor our communication with you, we may analyse the information you provide. If you’ve provided a postal address, we will contact you in this way with marketing we believe you’ll be interested in, unless you've told us not to. If you’d prefer us not to contact you by post, or if you’d like to change the way we process your data, let us know by contacting Supporter Services on: Phone: 0300 323 1216 Email: supporterservices@xunlei173.cn Post: Supporter Services Team, Battersea Dogs & Cats Home, 4 Battersea Park Road, London SW8 4AA. Further information about how we protect and use your personal data is set out in our Privacy Policy.


Keep in Touch

Only with your support can we continue to be here for every dog and cat. That's why we are asking you to let us keep in touch. By choosing to hear from Battersea and our trading company, you’ll receive behind the scenes news, updates about our animals, general information about rehoming, volunteering and various ways you can support Battersea, including fundraising, events and campaigning. You’re free to change your mind at any time.
