Get your free pet care guide

Just like your pet, Battersea is always here for you. We've put together a handy free pet care guide to help you give back to the pet who gives you so much.

No-one knows your pet the way you do, but with Battersea’s free pet care guide you’ll learn even more about keeping them safe and happy. This free guide is packed with expert advice and answers to all your questions on toxic foods, body language, training, and brain games for your pet. We’ve even included recipes and instructions for making pet-safe homemade treats and toys.

Your privacy rights

By submitting your telephone number, you consent to Battersea getting in touch with you to request delivery details, confirm your choice of cat or dog pet care guide, to ask you about supporting our work and how you would like to hear about our work in the future.

If you would prefer not to receive a call from us, you can still request your free pet care guide by contacting Supporter Services on: Phone: 0300 323 1216.

The data you’ve provided will be used by Battersea Dogs & Cats Home and Battersea Dogs’ Home Ltd to process your request and to continue to contact you with marketing we believe you’ll be interested in. This may include contacting you using telephone, SMS, post and/or email in line with the contact preferences you gave at the time of signing up.

To help us better understand our supporters, and to tailor our communication with you, we may analyse the information you provide. We sometimes use information from public sources.

If you’d like to change the way we process your data let us know by contacting Supporter Services on: Phone: 0300 323 1216 Email: supporterservices@xunlei173.cn Post: Supporter Services Team, Battersea Dogs & Cats Home, 4 Battersea Park Road, London SW8 4AA.

Further information about how we protect and use your personal data is set out in our Privacy Policy
