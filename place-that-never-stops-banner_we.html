How your support helps

Even on Christmas Day we make sure every animal who needs us has a warm, safe and loving place to stay. And Ali, who you may recognise from Paul O’Grady: For the Love of Dogs, hands out presents to every animal.

Boo's story

Hanging around London looking for scraps of food was Boo’s tough reality before some concerned people brought her into Battersea. She was given a health check where we discovered she was pregnant. Every animal arriving at Battersea gets a thorough examination upon arrival, even strays on Christmas day.

Archie's story

Archie’s owners made the responsible decision to bring him to Battersea after buying him online and not getting what they expected. We were able to give him the medical care and training he needed and Archie quickly found a new home.
