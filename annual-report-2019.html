Annual Report 2019

Since it was founded in 1860, Battersea has helped over three million dogs and cats and our work reaches well beyond our three centres to help thousands more animals, in the UK and all over the world. We couldn’t do it without our supporters who give their time and money so generously. Together we can improve the lives of the dogs and cats that urgently need our help. From all of us to all of you, thank you.
